Problem Context

Mobile app for helping young professionals (aged 22-30) manage and improve their mental health. The app should offer a blend of daily mental health exercises, progress tracking, and personalized content to help users reduce stress, enhance their mood, and cultivate mindfulness.

User Insights

Through interviews with 12 users aged 22-30, I discovered that 60% of Millenials and 80% of Gen Z professionals face menatal-health-related issues.

  • 9 in 12 says financial stress affects their mental wellbeing
  • 90% of them say their job is a major source of their stress
  • All of them says they lack enivironment or time to focus or unwind

Market Analysis

Competitive Landscape

Analysis of 8 leading mind wellness apps revealed that most prioritize feature quantity over user experience. My research showed opportunities to differentiate through simplicity and personalization.

Key Insights

Competitors focus heavily on promotional content, pushing users toward products rather than helping them. Users expressed frustration with this approach, wanting tools that serve their needs first.

Branding Strategy

Brand Colors

Style Guidelines

The visual identity emphasizes balance, relaxation, and tranquility. Teal is actually one of the most psychologically balanced and soothing colors in the spectrum, sitting between blue and green, both of which have well-documented mental and emotional effects.

Typography uses a contemporary sans-serif font family for excellent readability across all screen sizes. Consistent spacing and clear visual hierarchy guide users through complex financial information effortlessly.

Design Goals and Objectives

  1. Reduce stress and anxiety levels through guided mindfulness exercises
  2. Increase user consistency with personalized daily wellness routines
  3. Enhance focus and calmness via adaptive breathing and meditation sessions
  4. Create a soothing visual and auditory environment that promotes relaxation
  5. Guide users toward presence with personalized breathing and meditation journeys
