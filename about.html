Amal Anilkumar Here!

A 24 year old passionate UI/UX designer with a background in commerce and currently investing heavily in upskilling in UI/UX related fields.

I started my design story back in 2019. At that time, I was doing my Bachelor's and working as a freelancer, particularly with Cryptocurrency Marketing and Agents.

My job was simple at the time: creating digital assets, social media posts, marketing materials, and promotions. I didn’t know about UI or UX design yet, but I knew I loved design and felt passionate and excited when delivering more than what the client expected.

A simple work from my old desktop pc earned me descent pocket money a teenager can wish for, but I never knew about the filed's potential or mine.

As an average student from a mid tier family, I voted for the usual way of every aimless, directionless, unfocused drifter teenager. "Go with the flow".

After completing a bachelor's in commerce and a diploma in logistics and supply chain management (trending at that time) I flew to UAE in 2024 as an associate at Amazon.ae.

Life was going okay and saturated untill i met with a small accident to my spine which made standing and weight lifting job a nightmare. Which forced me to resign and came back to my home country.

That had me thinking about the jobs that dont need standing for long hours and got into digital marketing. as I was doing that my childhood friend (who always motivates me about my design skills but never took it serious) gave me the words one last time : "You are good at designing"

I found Google's UX course on coursera and decided to go for it. That was the literal turning point of my life. I finally discovered my passion. And here I'am. As a UI UX designer.

Now after 4 months of learning UI UX I felt an incompleteness. So i tried to study what i dont knew but needed for a UX designer.

Combining my past design knowledge and recent learning, my arsenal currently has:

Designing - Photoshop, Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, Canva.

Low Coding - Framer, Webflow, Quarkly.

Coding - Html, CSS, JS, Tailwind CSS,VS code,React.

With all this tools and skills I earned from my experience, I'm very confident and passionate that I can design.
